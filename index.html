About Fishing Fix

Land-based fishing stories, built for the wild edge.

Fishing Fix is a land-based fishing YouTuber built around remote Australia, rock fishing, catch-and-cook sessions, and the kind of patience you only get when the tide turns and the light drops low. The focus is simple: fish hard, film it clean, and tell the story with real atmosphere.

Big fish off the rocks, campfire meals, and the wild Australian wilderness have become the core of the brand. New films usually drop on Sundays, and each release is shaped like a short documentary rather than a quick clip.

What defines the brand

  • Land-based fishing and remote shoreline missions
  • Catch-and-cook stories from the Australian wild
  • Sunday film drops, rugged gear, and film-first pacing
